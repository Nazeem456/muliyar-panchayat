About Muliyar Panchayat

Muliyar Grama Panchayat is a local self-governance unit located in Bovikanam, Kerala. It is nestled in the Western Ghats region, known for its rich biodiversity, tribal communities, and lush green landscape.

The panchayat is committed to delivering quality services to its residents through transparent administration, proactive development planning, and community participation through Ward Sabhas and Grama Sabhas.

Key development areas include rural infrastructure, water supply, sanitation, women empowerment via Kudumbashree, and improving living standards for Scheduled Tribe communities.

Healthcare

Primary Health Centres & Sub-Centres

Government-run health facilities providing primary and preventive healthcare to all residents of Muliyar Panchayat.

🏥

Muliyar Primary Health Centre

The main PHC serving the panchayat with OPD services, maternal & child health, immunisation, and emergency care. Staffed by Medical Officer, nurses, and paramedics.

📍 Muliyar Junction  |  ⏰ 8AM – 5PM
🩺

Sub-Centre – Panamaram Road

Health sub-centre providing ante-natal care, immunisation sessions, and basic outpatient services for the northern wards of the panchayat.

📍 Panamaram Road  |  ⏰ 9AM – 4PM
🧪

Sub-Centre – Tribal Colony Area

Dedicated sub-centre for tribal communities providing mobile medical camps, ASHA worker coordination, and regular health monitoring programmes.

📍 Colony Area  |  ⏰ 9AM – 3PM
💊

Jan Aushadhi Kendram

Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana outlet offering generic medicines at subsidised prices to BPL and general public.

📍 Panchayat Building  |  ⏰ 9AM – 5PM
🌡️

Aarogyakeralam Programme

Comprehensive health screening and follow-up programme covering NCD (diabetes, hypertension, cancer) detection for all adults above 30 years.

📍 PHC & Sub-Centres  |  Regular Camps
🚑

108 Ambulance & Emergency

DISHA helpline and 108 ambulance service coordinated through the PHC for emergency response within the panchayat limits.

📞 Dial 108 / DISHA 1056  |  24×7
